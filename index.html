Biography [CV]

I am currently a research assistant in the School of Data Scicence, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Ruimao Zhang. Previously, I received the MSc degree from School of Electronics and Computer Science, University of Southampton in 2020, and B. Eng degree from College of Engineering, South China Agriculture University in 2019.

My research interest lies in deep learning and multimodality learning, including scene understanding, visual reasoning, and neural-symbolic learning.
